Gameplay

Starz Puzzle is a strategy game where you have a limited amount of time to collect all the stars on a given level. Some blocks will hinder you. Make smart moves to clear your path.

Warning! You can only push blocks, not pull them.
The longer it takes you to collect stars, the lower the points you are getting.
Hurry up, clock is ticking!
